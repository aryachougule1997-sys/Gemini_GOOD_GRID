The Good Grid
AI-Powered Location-Based Trust & Impact Platform

1. Introduction
Modern communities face a growing disconnect between individuals willing to contribute and organizations that need local, reliable support. While volunteering platforms exist, most lack local relevance, trust verification, real-world accountability, and measurable impact tracking. On the other hand, individuals—especially students—struggle to convert meaningful real-world contributions into visible skills, trust scores, and career value.
The Good Grid addresses this gap by building a location-based, AI-powered task ecosystem where individuals, students, NGOs, and organizations collaborate on real-world tasks. The platform transforms local contributions into verified impact, trust metrics, gamified growth, and resume-ready achievements.

2. Problem Statement
